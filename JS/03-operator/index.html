Comparison Operators

Compare two values and return true or false

== (Equal)

5 == "5"

=== (Strict Equal)

5 === "5"

!= (Not Equal)

5 != 3

!== (Strict Not Equal)

5 !== "5"

> (Greater Than)

10 > 5

< (Less Than)

5 < 10

>= (Greater or Equal)

10 >= 10

<= (Less or Equal)

5 <= 10

Assignment Operators

Assign values to variables

= (Assign)

x = 10

+= (Add & Assign)

x += 5

-= (Subtract & Assign)

x -= 3

*= (Multiply & Assign)

x *= 2

/= (Divide & Assign)

x /= 2

%= (Modulus & Assign)

x %= 3
